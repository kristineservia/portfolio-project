Intro

My name is Kristine Servia. I'm originally from San Juan, Puerto Rico, and currently live in Lake Mary, Florida. I studied art and design in college, then went to graduate school to further study visual art. I make drawings, paper sculptures, and use Adobe programs in the process of making work.

About me

I'm learning Web Development because I'm also passionate about learning how to build websites and software applications.
